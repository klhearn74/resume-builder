Questions

Before you start

Won't this get flagged as being written by AI?
What actually gives away AI-written resumes is generic phrasing that could belong to anyone — "proven track record," "results-driven," "fast-paced environment." That's exactly why ResumeFit bans that kind of filler outright. Every rewrite stays anchored to your real job titles, your own words, and your actual experience, so it reads like you — not a template.
Is my resume information safe?
Yes. Your resume details are saved only in your own browser on your own device — they're never stored on a server or seen by anyone else. Closing your browser or clearing its data removes them completely.
Will ResumeFit make up experience I don't have?
No — this is a hard rule we built in. ResumeFit only reframes and strengthens what's already in your resume. It will never add a skill, job duty, or achievement that isn't genuinely yours, even if it would raise your score.
Does this work for any industry?
Yes. ResumeFit works from your actual resume and the actual job posting, so it adapts to whatever field you're in — retail, trades, healthcare, hospitality, tech, and everything in between.
Why not just ask an AI chatbot myself?
You can, but a general chatbot won't give you a structured ATS score, won't reliably catch itself fabricating details, and won't hand you a formatted resume and cover letter ready to send. ResumeFit is purpose-built for this one job, with guardrails a generic chat conversation doesn't have.
What if my experience spans two different fields?
Upload both resumes and ResumeFit blends them into one resume tailored to the job — no manual copy-pasting between documents needed. It reads as one coherent background, not two resumes stapled together.
